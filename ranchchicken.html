Ranch Chicken

This recipe is great for on the go individuals. This is something with minimal ingredients that you can set and forget. Kids will love it with a side of rice or pasta.

Ingredients
  • 3 lbs of Chicken Breast
  • 1 box of Cream Cheese
  • 6 tbs of ranch seasoning
  • 1 can of cream of chicken
Steps
  1. Get a crockpot out
  2. Mix together all ingredients in your crockpot
  3. Set crockpot on low and let it cook for 8 hours
  4. After 8 hours make a serving of rice or noodles to put the crack chicken on
  5. Mix crockpot one more time and then scoop onto noodles or rice and enjoy
