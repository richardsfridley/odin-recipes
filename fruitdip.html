Fruit Dip

This dip is a sweet way to add that extra sweetnes to the fruit that you are enjoying at home. Kids love this and it turns that dessert after dinner time into a little bit more of a healthy adventure.

Ingredients
  • 1 jar of Marshamallow Fluff
  • 1 box of Cream Cheese
Steps
  1. Grab a mixing bowl larger than the cream cheese and marshamallow combined
  2. Open both packages and mix together
  3. Cut up fruit of your choice
  4. Enjoy by dipping fruit into bowl
