Ben's Amazing Lasagna

Ben's amazing lasagna is a scrumptious recipe that only uses the best ingredient's. The creation of this recipe goes back to the origins of Richards family italian heritage.

Ingredients
  • 1/4 Cup Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 Cup of Ricotta Cheese
  • 1lb Hamburger
  • 1lb Italian Sausage
  • 1 box of Lasagana Noodles
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 jar of Spaghetti Sauce
Steps
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Brown the hamburger and italian sausage together, while draining the excess grease.
  3. While you are browning the meat mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and mozzarella together.
  4. Cook the noodles until tender
  5. Once the meat is browned and cooled a bit. Add the meat to the cheese mix stirring together.
  6. Pour an 1/8 cup of sauce into the bottom of the pan and then cover it with noodles.
  7. Put a layer of cheese mixture, then sauce, then noodles and repeat 3 times.
  8. Bake for 50 minutes at 350 degrees
